Privacy Policy for ScopeForge Pro

Last updated: May 2025

ScopeForge Pro is a browser extension that processes user input using the OpenAI API. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

What We Collect

  • Brief Content: Submitted by the user, processed via the OpenAI API to generate a Scope of Work. Not stored or logged.
  • License Key: Stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local for access control. Not transmitted externally.

What We Don't Collect

  • No names, emails, or personal identifiers are collected
  • No tracking, analytics, or cookies are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

We use the OpenAI API to generate content. OpenAI does not store or use your data for training. Learn more at OpenAI Policies.
